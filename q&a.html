What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition where participants come together in a short time (1 day) to develop a project related to the theme of the event. It's a fun and competitive way to learn computer science, software development, and problem-solving!

Who can attend?

All middle and high school students in Georgia are welcome to attend! No prior experience is required.

How much does it cost?

Admission is completely free! Participants should sign up to secure their spot before the event.

What if I don't know how to code?

That's perfectly okay! Hackathons are a great way to learn, meet new people, and gain experience. We'll offer beginner-friendly workshops and also cover more advanced concepts for those who want a challenge.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! We'll have 1st/2nd/3rd place prizes plus special awards (e.g., most creative, best solo project, and more). Prizes are sponsored by State Farm and other amazing sponsors.

What's the schedule?

HackGwinnett 5 is on October 25, 2025 from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The day includes opening ceremonies, workshops, hacking time, project submissions, and an awards ceremony.

Will food be provided?

Yes! We'll provide snacks and refreshments throughout the day. Specific meal information will be shared closer to the event.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and a positive attitude! We recommend bringing headphones and any other tools you prefer for coding.
