Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about HackGwinnett? Start here. Can’t find what you need? Contact us.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition where participants come together in a short time (1 day) to develop a project related to the theme of the event. It's a fun and competitive way to learn computer science, software development, and problem-solving!

Who can attend?

All middle and high school students in Georgia are welcome to attend!

How much does it cost?

Admission is free, but participants should sign up to secure their spot before the event.

What if I don’t know how to code?

That's okay! Hackathons are a great way to learn, meet new people, and gain experience. We’ll offer beginner-friendly workshops and also cover more advanced concepts for those who want a challenge.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! We’ll have 1st/2nd/3rd place prizes plus special awards (e.g., most creative, best solo project, and more).

How do teams work?

You can work solo or in a team of up to three people—your choice!
