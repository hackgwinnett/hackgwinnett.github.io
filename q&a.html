Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition where participants come together in a short time (1 day) to develop a project related to the theme of the event. It's a fun and competitive way to learn computer science concepts, software development, and problem-solving!

Who can attend?

All middle and high school students in Georgia are welcome to attend!

How much does it cost?

Admission is free, but participants will need to sign up to secure their spot before the event.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! We will have traditional first, second, and third place prizes as well as prizes for the most creative projects, best solo projects, and more.

How do teams work?

Participants can choose to work in a team of up to three people, but solo projects are welcome too!
