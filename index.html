Hack-a-What?

HackGwinnett features mentors, workshops, and prizes, and strives to build an inclusive environment for individuals interested in technology. They run Panel Series and HackFest events for younger kids interested in technology.

HackGwinnett's signature event is its annual hackathon, free of cost, hosted at GSMST for middle school and high school students all over GA. Here, students gather to create innovative solutions to real-world problems within a short time constraint.

Be on the lookout for HackGwinnett's fourth hackathon on October 19th to build awesome projects, participate in enriching workshops, and make new friends!
