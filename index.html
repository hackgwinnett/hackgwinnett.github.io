Hack-a-What?

HackGwinnett features mentors, workshops, prizes, and strives to build an inclusive environment for individuals interested in technology. They run Panel Series and HackFest events for younger kids interested in technology. HackGwinnett's signature event is their annual hackathon, free of cost, hosted at GSMST for high school students all over Metro Atlanta. A hackathon is a competition for students to gather and create innovative solutions to real-world problems within a certain time constraint. Be on the lookout for HackGwinnett's second hackathon in October to build awesome projects, participate in enriching workshops, and make new friends!
